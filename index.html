Rearrange Reader





Rearrange Reader is a simple Javascript experiment designed to test the readability of the apocryphal Cambridge letter substitution challenge. It goes something like this:

"Aoccdrnig to rscheearch at Cmabrigde Uinervtisy, it deosn't mttaer in waht oredr the ltteers in a wrod are, the olny iprmoetnt tihng is taht the frist and lsat ltteer be at the rghit pclae. The rset can be a toatl mses and you can sitll raed it wouthit a porbelm. Tihs is bcuseae the huamn mnid deos not raed ervey lteter by istlef, but the wrod as a wlohe."

Can you read it? Some can, some can't. Try it yourself, above. Copy in any passage and hit "Cipher It!" Try to read what you've scrambled and make your own theories as to how it works (or doesn't).
